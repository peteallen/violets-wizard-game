Asset type: isolated canonical full-body identity source for a premium
children's storybook adventure game.

Create exactly one complete original magical railway conductor, standing in a
relaxed front three-quarter pose and looking warmly toward a young passenger.
Show the entire figure from cap to both boot soles, centered with generous cyan
padding and no cast shadow. He is a genial British man in his late fifties with
a sturdy average build, a weathered rosy-brown face, kind hazel eyes, silvering
dark sideburns, a neat short moustache, and a reassuring half-smile. His
expression says that he has helped many nervous first-years find the right
train and still enjoys the moment.

Give him an original plum-and-charcoal railway uniform: a fitted knee-length
coat with brass piping and large practical pockets, a warm cream waistcoat,
dark trousers, scuffed oxblood boots, and a soft peaked cap with one small
unlettered brass star. One gloved hand holds a plain brass pocket watch on a
short chain at waist level; the other hand rests open and welcoming beside him.
Keep both hands anatomically coherent, naturally sized, attached to their
sleeves, and fully visible. Do not add a whistle to his mouth, luggage, train,
platform, ticket, wand, weapon, or readable insignia.

Match reference 1's richly painted opaque gouache-and-watercolor picture-book
finish: visible paper tooth, soft dark-brown contour accents dissolving into
paint, organic asymmetry, woven cloth grain, rubbed leather, warm upper-left
gold light, and cool violet-blue shadow masses. Use expressive storybook adult
proportions with a modestly enlarged head, believable shoulders and joints, and
both feet planted on one level ground line. He must be an original illustration
with no resemblance to a real actor and no film-specific costume.

Place the figure on reference 2's perfectly flat uniform solid cyan field. The
background has no gradient, texture, floor plane, shadow, reflection, scenery,
steam, decoration, or lighting variation. Never use cyan in the figure. No
extra people, animals, duplicate limbs, cropped parts, floating objects, text,
letters, numbers, logo, watermark, signature, border, panel, anime, vector art,
3D rendering, plastic skin, pure black, or hard ink outlines. Return only the
one complete conductor on cyan.
