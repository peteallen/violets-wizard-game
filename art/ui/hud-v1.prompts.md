# Violet's Field Kit HUD v1 production prompts

All assets in this set are textless transparent material layers. The runtime
owns contact shadows, objective pulse, golden-thread guidance, reduced-motion
behavior, item ownership, enabled state, wand selection, and magical effects.

Every source was generated on a perfectly flat solid `#00ff00` chroma-key
background with no background shadow, gradient, texture, reflection, floor, or
lighting variation. The key color does not appear in the painted subject.

## Closed satchel

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: transparent-background diegetic HUD prop for Violet's Wizard Game

Create one richly hand-painted closed school travel satchel that replaces a
flat vector HUD icon and visibly belongs to the same premium Field Kit as
Violet's detailed open satchel. Show a compact blackberry-leather satchel almost
straight-on with a slight storybook three-quarter turn: sturdy curved handle,
irregular hand-shaped flap, layered side gussets, plum stitching, worn rubbed
leather grain, and a small aged-brass clasp embossed with a simple original owl
face. Use practical child-sized school-bag proportions.

Use premium children's magical-school storybook gouache and colored pencil,
richly tactile rather than flat vector, with visible canvas tooth, fine leather
texture, dark-brown variable-weight ink, softened handmade edges, and layered
base-shadow-highlight construction. Use deep blackberry and plum leather,
muted burgundy stitching, aged brass, and warm brown ink under a warm upper-left
honey key light.

Square artboard. Exactly one isolated satchel centered and filling about 78
percent of the canvas, with a complete silhouette and generous padding. No
words, labels, logo, watermark, background scene, character, hand, compass,
wand, book, loose paper, glow, sparkle, external cast shadow, status badge,
button ring, circle backplate, software icon, perfect geometry, pure black, or
pure white.
