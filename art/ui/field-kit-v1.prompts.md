# Violet's Field Kit v1 production prompts

All assets in this set are textless painted material bases. The runtime owns
every visible word, character, portrait, route, progress value, selection, and
save-dependent state.

## Painted title backdrop

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: textless 16:9 full-screen title backdrop for Violet's Wizard Game

Create a richly hand-painted children's wizard-school storybook title painting
that replaces a flat code-drawn castle backdrop while preserving the existing
live title, envelope action, Violet, and owl overlays.

Show a wondrous original magical boarding-school castle across a dark
reflective lake at welcoming night, viewed straight-on like a layered
theater-stage diorama. Place a large warm imperfect moon in the upper-left sky.
Irregular towers and bridges fill the middle distance, with candlelit windows,
mist ribbons over the lake, textured foreground shore and stones, and a few
reeds. The architecture must be original rather than copied from a film or
existing castle.

Use premium painterly gouache and colored pencil on visible paper/canvas tooth,
richly detailed but uncluttered, with soft edges, tactile brush texture,
hand-shaped forms, and warm storybook magic rather than film realism or flat
vector art. Preserve a calm dark central upper zone for the live two-line title,
a calm central-lower zone for the large envelope action, and an unobstructed
lower-right stage area for the live child and owl. Use deep night blue,
twilight violet, candle gold, honey, aged stone, and oak with no pure black or
white.

Background only. No people, children, owls, creatures, characters, envelopes,
buttons, UI panels, labels, text, letters, numbers, logos, crests, watermarks,
perfect geometry, modern software chrome, photorealism, flat vector areas, or
hard black outlines.

## Objective reminder

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: textless painted objective-reminder prop for Violet's Wizard Game

Create one richly hand-painted, wide horizontal objective reminder that can sit
compactly along the upper edge of a magical storybook room. It is a single
physical parchment field note with an irregular deckled silhouette, backed by
shallow blackberry leather visible around parts of the top and lower edge, with
subtle plum stitching and a few small aged-brass mounting details.

Leave the left quarter calm and open for a live ornate brass compass. Leave the
broad center-right quiet and blank for a live two-to-three-word caption. Keep
the far upper-right uncluttered for a live wax close seal. The runtime owns all
three of those layers; the painting is only their richly made physical holder.

Use premium children's magical-school storybook gouache and colored pencil at
the same tactile quality tier as Violet's painted satchel: visible paper and
leather grain, hand-shaped edges, dark-brown variable-weight ink, rubbed folds,
deckled fibers, tarnished brass, and layered base-shadow-highlight construction.
Use warm cream and honey parchment, blackberry and plum leather, muted burgundy
stitching, aged brass, and dark-brown ink under a warm upper-left key light.

Isolate the complete prop on a perfectly flat solid `#00ff00` chroma-key
background with generous padding. The background has no shadow, gradient,
texture, reflection, floor, or lighting variation, and `#00ff00` does not appear
in the prop. No external cast shadow.

No words, letters, numbers, labels, logos, watermark, compass, star, needle,
wax seal, X, owl, character, portrait, button, ribbon, scroll ends, software
panel, perfect rectangle, perfect circle, or action-like decorative ornament.
