## Prompt

Input images: Image 1 is the identity and world reference: preserve the recognizable original wizard-school castle as the same place, including its irregular many-towered stone mass, tall graceful central spire, steep slate roofs, bridges, terraces, warm inhabited windows, rugged Scottish setting, and premium gouache-and-watercolor finish. Recompose it into a new elevated autumn overview rather than copying the lake-vista camera angle. Image 2 is the material and interface-finish reference: borrow its softly mottled golden parchment, plum-violet surround, restrained antique-gold warmth, paper tooth, and hand-painted Field Kit richness, but do not depict an open book, page seam, leather cover, metal corner fittings, or any interface frame.
Primary request: Paint a beautiful elevated oblique overview of the same original magical castle and its broad autumn grounds at twilight. The castle belongs along the upper third as a distant scenic anchor, surrounded by russet and plum woodland, amber lawns, rocky terraces, a small dark-blue lake edge, and violet mountain layers. Treat the whole landscape as a richly painted parchment illustration rather than a literal plan or diagram. Warm honey-gold windows and autumn foliage glow gently against twilight violet shadows.
Composition/framing: Exact widescreen 16:9 landscape, level and intentionally spacious. Keep the broad central horizontal field from roughly one-fifth to four-fifths of the image height calm, low-contrast, and lightly detailed so five live destination panels and their route graphics can remain readable across it. Push the castle silhouette and strongest detail toward the upper third; use soft terrain washes, sparse low foliage, and quiet parchment texture through the central field. Preserve visual interest near the outer edges without creating five implied slots, a route, or a diagram. No border and no central book gutter.
Style/medium: Finished premium children's storybook environment painting in layered gouache and watercolor on tactile aged parchment, with visible paper tooth, feathered pigment edges, subtle dry-brush stone and foliage detail, softened dark-brown contours, luminous gold accents, and atmospheric depth. Scenic and immersive, not cartographic, icon-like, flat, vector, photorealistic, 3D, or generic concept art.
Lighting/mood: Cozy early-autumn twilight, welcoming discovery, quiet magic, and the feeling of opening a treasured field guide. Warm gold and muted copper highlights against plum, aubergine, indigo, and twilight-violet shadows.
Constraints: Textless opaque artwork. No labels, words, letters, numbers, legends, compass roses, routes, paths drawn as routes, dotted lines, arrows, markers, pins, stars, checks, badges, Here or Next state, fog-of-war state, destination panels, portrait frames, portraits, people, children, adults, faces, silhouettes, hands, creatures, animals, birds, owls, toads, ghosts, boats, UI, buttons, tabs, page seam, book hardware, readable signs, banners, coats of arms, flags, logos, watermark, signature, baked sparkles, giant moon, horror lighting, pure black, copied film architecture, or copied camera angle.
