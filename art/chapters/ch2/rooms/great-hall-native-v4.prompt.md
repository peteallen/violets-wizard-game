## Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: final full-screen 16:9 gameplay room painting for Chapter Two of a premium children's storybook adventure.
Input images: Image 1 is composition-only: retain its useful empty central aisle, left entrance, distant staff dais, long side tables, plain stool, and starry blue ceiling concept, but replace the simplified line-art finish and redesign the room with richer original architecture. Image 2 is the warm timber/stone material detail, deep interior lighting, and premium finish reference. Image 3 is the mysterious academic depth, bookish surface detail, and blue-violet shadow reference.
Primary request: Paint an original vast magical school dining hall prepared for first-year welcome, Sorting, and feast. Soaring honey-gray stone walls and dark timber roof ribs frame an enchanted deep-blue star ceiling. Many warm floating candles hang at varied depths. Long dark-oak tables with empty golden place settings flank a broad open central aisle. A staff dais and high windows anchor the far wall. A clear pointed doorway enters from far left. Near the front-center place one plain, low, three-legged wooden stool, small enough not to block the aisle. Walls and columns use only nonfigurative geometric stone corbels and plain brass shelves.
Composition/framing: exact widescreen 16:9 stage-like room view, level child-height camera, no fisheye. Keep the lower quarter and central aisle calm and open for Violet, the Hat, teachers, and multiple separately rendered children. Side tables should frame the action without crowding it. Preserve readable depth from front aisle to dais.
Style/medium: Match Chapter One's dense premium storybook painting, layered gouache-and-watercolor appearance, fine stone/wood/brass/wax texture, visible paper tooth, softly broken dark-brown contours, luminous candle pools, deep ink-blue and violet recesses, reflected gold along the floor, and subtle atmospheric depth. It must have the same finished richness and dimensional lighting as the references, not simplified concept art, flat cartoon linework, cel shading, or a low-detail wash.
Mood: awe-inspiring but warm, ceremonial, child-safe, and expectant.
Constraints: absolutely no people, silhouettes, animals, birds, ghosts, portraits, statues, carved creatures, gargoyles, creature-shaped brackets, faces, heads, hats, banners, heraldry, letters, numbers, logos, watermark, signature, copied film details, photorealism, 3D rendering, flat vector art, interaction effects, baked sparkles, or character shadows. Keep all decorations nonfigurative.
