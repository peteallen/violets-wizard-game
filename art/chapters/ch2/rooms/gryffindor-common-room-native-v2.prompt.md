Style/medium: Match Chapter One's dense premium storybook painting, layered gouache-and-watercolor appearance, fine stone/oak/brass/woven-fabric texture, visible paper tooth, softly broken dark-brown contours, coherent amber candle-and-hearth glow, deep violet-blue window shadows, scuffed wood, and subtle atmospheric depth. Mood: safe, celebratory, lived-in, and specifically Violet's new home.
Constraints: no children, adults, faces, silhouettes, hands, animals, birds, owls, lions, pets, portraits with people, statues, readable books, signs, labels, letters, numbers, coats of arms, logos, watermark, signature, copied movie set, photorealism, 3D rendering, flat vector art, pure black, excessive clutter, throne-like furniture, hard spotlight, interaction sparkles, or character shadows.
