## Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: final full-screen 16:9 flagship gameplay vista for Chapter Two of a premium children's storybook adventure.
Input images: Image 1 is composition-only: preserve the useful clear lake foreground, lower-left empty boat rim, and distant centered castle idea, but do not preserve its simplified drawing style or exact castle design. Image 2 is the direct finish, atmosphere, fine-detail, dusk palette, luminous-brass, and premium storybook-quality reference. Image 3 is the warm inhabited-light versus deep violet-shadow reference. Create new original architecture.
Primary request: Paint a breathtaking original night vista across a broad dark lake. On the far shore, a many-towered ancient Scottish magical school castle rises from a rugged wooded cliff, grand but with an original storybook silhouette. Hundreds of tiny honey-gold windows make it warm and inhabited. Slender towers, steep roofs, bridges, terraces, and irregular stone masses step toward one graceful central tower. The castle and its long broken golden reflection are the focal point, centered slightly above the middle. Behind it, indigo mountain layers soften into a velvet blue sky with thin violet clouds and a modest crescent moon.
Composition/framing: exact widescreen 16:9 view, level camera. Foreground lower quarter is a calm near-black-blue water plane with a low empty wooden landing edge and the curved rim of one empty rowboat entering subtly from lower left. Keep center foreground open for separately rendered boat occupants and water animation. Castle should feel distant, deep, and monumental without resembling a film still.
Style/medium: Match Chapter One's dense premium storybook painting: layered watercolor sky and water, detailed gouache castle stone and foliage, visible paper tooth, fine material detail, atmospheric depth, softly broken dark-brown contours, warm window and moon glints, blue-violet shadow masses, and broken dry-brush reflections. It must have the finished richness and dimensional lighting of Image 2, not simplified concept art, flat cartoon linework, cel shading, generic matte-painting realism, or a low-detail wash.
Mood: quiet awe, safety, arrival, and the beginning of Violet's own adventure.
Constraints: no students, teachers, boaters, faces, silhouettes, animals, birds, owls, fish, statues, flying shapes, ghosts, flags, coats of arms, readable signs, letters, numbers, logos, watermark, signature, copied castle architecture or camera angle, photorealism, 3D rendering, flat vector art, pure black, horror lighting, lightning, storm, giant moon, fireworks, magical beams, baked sparkles, or character shadows.
