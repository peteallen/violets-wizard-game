## Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: final full-screen 16:9 gameplay room painting for Chapter Two of a premium children's storybook adventure.
Input images: Image 1 is the direct style, finish, railway atmosphere, and material-quality reference. Image 2 is the stone-floor and brick texture reference. Image 3 is the warm interior depth and amber-versus-violet lighting reference. Preserve the same premium painted richness, nuanced surfaces, dimensional light, and dark-brown edge language, but create a new platform composition.
Primary request: Paint an original secret British railway platform at the start of a wondrous school journey. A magnificent deep scarlet steam locomotive with warm brass fittings waits along the left and middle distance, its rounded boiler and burgundy passenger carriages partly veiled by soft cream steam. No number, crest, nameplate, or lettering. Honey-red brick arches, dark teal ironwork, warm lamps, stacked school trunks, and a few empty luggage trolleys create a rich magical station without clutter. The far right must remain architecturally open and calm for a friendly conductor overlay, with a clear route toward the train.
Composition/framing: exact widescreen 16:9 stage-like room view, level child-height camera, no fisheye. Keep a wide unobstructed stone platform across the lower quarter, with Violet entering from the left and enough calm floor for two characters to face each other. Train should feel enormous and exciting but not crop its recognizable front silhouette. Keep all props at the far edges.
Style/medium: Match the Chapter One references' dense premium storybook painting, layered gouache-and-watercolor appearance, fine brick/iron/brass/leather texture, visible paper tooth, softly broken dark-brown contours, luminous lamp and steam light, deep navy and twilight-violet recesses, and subtle atmospheric depth. Not simplified concept art, flat cartoon linework, cel shading, or a low-detail wash.
Mood: secret, welcoming, wondrous, and poised for departure.
Constraints: no people, conductors, faces, silhouettes, animals, pets, train crew, statues, readable tickets, signs, clocks, platform numbers, labels, letters, logos, watermark, signature, copied film composition, photorealism, 3D rendering, flat vector art, pure black, modern displays, harsh smoke, baked sparkles, or character shadows.
